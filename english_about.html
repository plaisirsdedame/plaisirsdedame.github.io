    OUR story

    Boutique Plaisirs de Dame, located in the heart of the village of Mont-Tremblant, is a family-owned business with the goal of providing a refined selection of high-quality ready-to-wear for the contemporary woman and a shopping experience tailored to our region.

    At Plaisirs de Dame, we believe in elegant simplicity and the authenticity of style!

    Our carefully curated collection features pieces that are both modern and timeless, catering to women of all styles:

    2 to 18 years old; TP (very small) to XXL (very very large), XS to 2XL

    Since 2011, we have been committed to providing a personalized and intimate experience.

    During your visit, you will discover creations from locally and internationally acclaimed brands known for their quality and finesse.

    "Plaisirs de Dame is more than just a boutique; it's an invitation to celebrate your individuality and to indulge in pleasure.
    We eagerly await your visit to Mont-Tremblant for a unique and memorable shopping experience."

    Sonia Malhioud - Owner
